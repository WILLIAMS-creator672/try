THE BOYS' BRIGADE IN AKWA IBOM STATE.

We briefly welcome you to the BOYS' BRIGADE NIGERIA, AKWA IBOM STATE COUNCIL where we help to build boys and train them in the way of Christ according to our object that says "The Advancement of Christ Kingdom".

On 23rd September, 1987, the Military Government of General Ibrahim Badamosi Babangida through a Nation - Wide Broadcast created Akwa Ibom State out of the Old Cross River State. By that pronouncements ,all Machinery of Government and Organizations were transferred to the new State. The Boys' Brihad Nigeria was not left out.
